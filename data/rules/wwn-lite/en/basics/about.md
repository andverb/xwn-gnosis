## Information

### Base System
WWN-Lite is based on **Worlds Without Number** by Kevin Crawford ([Sine Nomine Publishing](https://www.drivethrurpg.com/browse/pub/3482/Sine-Nomine-Publishing)).

- [WWN System Reference Document](https://www.drivethrurpg.com/en/product/473939/worlds-without-number-system-reference-document)
- [WWN Free Edition](https://www.drivethrurpg.com/product/348809/Worlds-Without-Number-Free-Edition)


### WWN-Lite
This system is condensed quick-reference version of original WWN, with some homebrew by **u/KaiokenXTen**.

- [WWN-Lite on gdrive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1zRU7VL6S4izrw-yN-gBqh35wrrnFkc2y)

### Homebrew Changes (this version)

**XP & Leveling**

- Alternative silver-for-XP system: 1 XP per $p of treasure recovered & spent frivolously

**Attributes & Skills**

- Wis can replace Dex for aimed weapons; Int/Craft for lockpicking; Dex or Int for Initiative (reduce dominance of Dex)
- Work skill removed; Alchemy skill added (Experts can craft Alchemical Works)
- Developed Attribute focus capped at +2 modifier

**Classes & Magic**

- Max HP at level 1 (optional)
- No dual Art classes (less class charts, less decision paralysis/tracking with 2 Effort pools/Art lists)
- Bards, Beastmasters, Clerics, Warlocks gain 2 Arts at odd levels (same amount of Arts in total, less charts)
- Bards are half-Mages (not half-Experts), but keep non-magic restrictions
- Impervious Defense nerfed: 14 + ½ level (was 15)
- Cantrip ability for spellcasting Mages replaces Elemental Sparks (minor effect 1/session)
- Renamed spells for brevity (credit: u/fireally)

**Equipment & Combat**

- Black powder firearms (flavor)
- Homebrew Casting enchantment added for War Shirts (unhindered spellcasting)
- Reduced boat/weapon options; adjusted ship speeds & combat
- Ship HP ×3, siege damage ×3 (same mechanics but maybe better clarity); simplified ship ranges (Sighted/Far/Near)
- Replaced mass combat rules with Crew combat rules

**Procedures**

- Added some delving & dungeon mapping procedures
- Extrapolated visibility rules from invisibility spells
- Encounter checks simplified: every 2, 3, or 4 turns

**Content**

- Added some Foci from AotLE to buffs Experts
- Added Homebrew Verderer (Druid) tradition
